Besan ke Chille

👤 Serves 2 ⏱ Prep 10 min 🔥 Cook 15 min
💜 14g protein/serving 🔥 ~220 cal
Ingredients
  • 1 cup besan (chickpea flour)
  • ¾ cup water
  • ½ small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp oil per chilla
  • Mint chutney: mint, cilantro, chili, lemon, salt
Instructions
  1. Whisk besan with water until no lumps remain
  2. Mix in onion, chili, cilantro, turmeric, cumin, and salt
  3. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat, add ½ tsp oil
  4. Pour a ladleful of batter and spread into a thin 7-inch circle
  5. Cook 2–3 min until edges lift and bottom is golden brown
  6. Flip and cook 1–2 more minutes
  7. Repeat for remaining batter
  8. Blend chutney ingredients until smooth; serve alongside
Meal Prep Tip

Make the full batch on Sunday. Refrigerate in a container. Reheat at 45 sec in microwave for Tuesday's office breakfast.

Overnight Oats Jar

👤 Serves 1 ⏱ Prep 5 min 🌙 Chill overnight
💜 22g protein 🔥 ~380 cal
Ingredients
  • ½ cup rolled oats (Bob's Red Mill)
  • ½ cup Fage 2% Greek yogurt
  • ½ cup whole milk
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tsp raw honey
  • ½ cup mixed berries (fresh or frozen)
  • Pinch of cinnamon (optional)
Instructions
  1. Add oats to a mason jar or lidded container
  2. Add Greek yogurt, milk, chia seeds, and honey
  3. Stir well to combine all ingredients
  4. Top with mixed berries
  5. Seal and refrigerate at least 6 hours (overnight)
  6. In the morning, stir and enjoy cold or gently warmed
Meal Prep Tip

Prep 2 jars on Sunday night (for Mon) and Tuesday night (for Wed). Takes 5 minutes total.

Quinoa Veggie Lunch Bowl

👤 Serves 3 (batch) ⏱ Prep 10 min 🔥 Cook 20 min
💜 22g protein/serving 🔥 ~420 cal
Ingredients (3 servings)
  • 1½ cups dry quinoa (Ancient Harvest)
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained & rinsed
  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • ½ cup feta crumbles (Athenos)
  • 3 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning, salt & pepper
Instructions
  1. Rinse quinoa, cook in 3 cups water — bring to boil, reduce heat, simmer 15 min
  2. Toss chickpeas with 1 tbsp oil, salt, and cumin; spread on baking sheet
  3. Roast chickpeas at 400°F for 18–20 min until crispy
  4. Let quinoa cool slightly, then divide into 3 meal-prep containers
  5. Top each with spinach, tomatoes, cucumber, roasted chickpeas, feta
  6. Whisk remaining olive oil + lemon juice; keep dressing in small container on side
  7. Refrigerate. Add dressing just before eating for best freshness.
Meal Prep Tip

Sunday batch yields 3 lunches (Mon, Tue, Wed). Total prep time: ~30 minutes for all 3.

Dal Tadka + Brown Rice

👤 Serves 3–4 ⏱ Prep 10 min 🔥 Cook 30 min
💜 18g protein/serving 🔥 ~380 cal
Ingredients
  • 1 cup toor dal (Swad or Laxmi)
  • 1 medium onion, finely diced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste, 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1½ cups brown rice (Lundberg)
  • Fresh cilantro to garnish
Instructions
  1. Wash dal and pressure cook with 3 cups water + turmeric (3–4 whistles or 20 min in pot)
  2. Cook brown rice separately per package (about 40 min)
  3. Heat ghee in a pan; add cumin seeds and let splutter
  4. Sauté onion until golden brown, ~8 minutes
  5. Add ginger-garlic paste, cook 2 minutes
  6. Add tomatoes, cook until soft and oil separates
  7. Add coriander, garam masala, and salt; cook 2 minutes
  8. Pour in cooked dal, stir well, adjust consistency with water
  9. Simmer 8–10 minutes; garnish with cilantro
  10. Serve hot over brown rice with a whole wheat roti

Paneer Tikka Wrap + Raita

👤 Serves 1 ⏱ Prep 15 min + 30 min marination 🔥 Cook 20 min
💜 28g protein 🔥 ~450 cal
Ingredients
  • 150g paneer, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • ½ cup plain yogurt (for marinade)
  • 1 tsp tandoori masala or tikka spice mix
  • ½ tsp cumin, ½ tsp coriander, ½ tsp chili powder
  • 1 bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • ½ red onion, cut into chunks
  • 1 large whole wheat tortilla (Mission Carb Balance)
  • Raita: ½ cup yogurt, ½ cucumber grated, cumin, salt, mint
Instructions
  1. Mix marinade: yogurt + tikka spice + cumin + coriander + chili + salt
  2. Coat paneer cubes, bell pepper, and onion in marinade
  3. Marinate at least 30 minutes (up to 8 hours in fridge)
  4. Thread on skewers or spread on a baking sheet
  5. Broil/bake at 425°F for 15–18 min, turning once, until charred edges appear
  6. Make raita: mix yogurt, grated cucumber (squeezed), cumin, salt, fresh mint
  7. Warm tortilla 20 seconds in microwave or on tawa
  8. Layer paneer tikka + veggies in tortilla, drizzle raita, roll tightly

Stuffed Aloo Paratha + Yogurt

👤 Makes 4 parathas ⏱ Prep 20 min 🔥 Cook 25 min
💜 18g protein (2 + yogurt) 🔥 ~420 cal
Ingredients
  • 2 cups chakki atta / whole wheat flour (Sujata or Pillsbury Gold)
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • 2 medium Yukon Gold potatoes, boiled & mashed
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds, ½ tsp amchur (dry mango powder)
  • ½ tsp red chili powder, 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 2 tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Salt to taste; ghee or butter for cooking
  • 1 cup plain whole-milk yogurt for serving
Instructions
  1. Knead atta with warm water into a soft, smooth dough; rest covered 20 min
  2. Mix mashed potatoes with cumin, amchur, chili, coriander, cilantro, and salt
  3. Divide dough into 4 equal balls
  4. Roll one ball into a 4-inch circle; place 2 tbsp filling in center
  5. Fold edges up and pinch closed, then gently roll into a 6-inch flat round
  6. Heat tawa on medium-high; place paratha and cook 2 min
  7. Flip, spread ½ tsp ghee, press edges down; cook another 2 min until golden spots appear
  8. Serve hot with a generous dollop of cold plain yogurt

Pasta with Tomato-Lentil Sauce

👤 Serves 2 ⏱ Prep 10 min 🔥 Cook 25 min
💜 22g protein/serving 🔥 ~480 cal
Ingredients
  • 2 cups whole wheat pasta (Barilla Protein+)
  • ½ cup red lentils (masoor dal), rinsed
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes (Rao's Homemade)
  • ½ onion, finely diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • ½ tsp red chili flakes, salt & pepper
  • 2 tbsp Parmesan (optional), fresh basil to finish
Instructions
  1. Simmer red lentils in 1½ cups water for 15 min until soft; set aside
  2. Cook pasta per package in salted water; drain, reserving ½ cup pasta water
  3. In a large pan, sauté onion in olive oil 5 min until translucent
  4. Add garlic, cook 1 minute until fragrant
  5. Add crushed tomatoes, Italian seasoning, chili flakes; simmer 8 min
  6. Stir in cooked lentils; simmer 5 more minutes
  7. Toss with drained pasta; loosen with pasta water if needed
  8. Top with Parmesan and fresh basil; serve immediately

Black Bean Veggie Burger Wrap

👤 Makes 2 patties ⏱ Prep 10 min 🔥 Cook 15 min
💜 20g protein/patty 🔥 ~430 cal
Ingredients
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained & rinsed (Bush's Best)
  • ¼ cup rolled oats (acts as binder)
  • ½ onion, finely diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin, ½ tsp smoked paprika, salt & pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 large whole wheat tortillas (Mission Carb Balance)
  • Avocado slices, baby spinach, salsa (Frontera)
Instructions
  1. Partially mash black beans in a bowl, leaving some whole for texture
  2. Mix in oats, onion, garlic, cumin, paprika, salt, pepper, and ½ tbsp oil
  3. Divide into 2 patties; refrigerate 10 min to firm up
  4. Heat remaining ½ tbsp oil in a skillet over medium heat
  5. Cook patties 4–5 min per side until golden crust forms
  6. Warm tortillas; layer with spinach, avocado, patty, and salsa
  7. Roll tightly and cut in half; serve immediately
Note

No eggs needed — the oats + bean starch bind perfectly. Patties keep refrigerated for 3 days.

Sunday Warm Grain Bowl

👤 Serves 2 ⏱ Prep 15 min 🔥 Cook 25 min
💜 20g protein/serving 🔥 ~460 cal
Ingredients
  • 1 cup dry quinoa (Ancient Harvest)
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained & rinsed
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • ½ red bell pepper, chunked
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil (California Olive Ranch)
  • 1 tsp cumin, ½ tsp garlic powder, salt & pepper
  • 2 cups arugula or baby spinach
  • ½ cup feta crumbles (Athenos)
  • Juice of 1 lemon; 1 tbsp tahini (optional dressing)
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (205°C)
  2. Toss chickpeas + veggies with 1½ tbsp olive oil, cumin, garlic powder, salt, pepper
  3. Spread on a baking sheet; roast 22–25 min until edges caramelize
  4. Rinse and cook quinoa (1 cup quinoa : 2 cups water; simmer 15 min, fluff)
  5. Divide quinoa between two bowls
  6. Top each with roasted veggies and chickpeas
  7. Add fresh arugula or spinach, crumble feta on top
  8. Squeeze lemon juice, drizzle with remaining olive oil or tahini
  9. Serve warm immediately
Meal Prep Tip

This is Saturday's dinner — also your Week 2 Sunday prep day. Double the quinoa to get a head start.
