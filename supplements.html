🔬 Supplement Details

Prenatal Vitamin with Methylfolate

💊 1 capsule daily · Morning with food
WhyMost critical preconception supplement. Prevents neural tube defects and supports cell division.
Dose1 capsule daily — do not double up
Best BrandRitual Essential Prenatal or Garden of Life Prenatal
Buy AtRitual.com, Target, Whole Foods, Amazon
Best TimeMorning with breakfast — take consistently at the same time each day
⚠️ Note: Look for methylfolate (not folic acid) — it's the bioavailable form that works for all body types, including those with MTHFR variants.

Algae-Based DHA Omega-3

💊 1 capsule daily · Morning with food
WhySupports egg quality and future baby brain & eye development. Essential since you don't eat fish.
Dose200–300mg DHA daily (check label)
Best BrandNordic Naturals Algae DHA
Buy AtNordicNaturals.com, Whole Foods, iHerb, Amazon
Best TimeMorning with your fattiest meal for best absorption
✅ Note: Algae-based DHA is identical to fish-derived DHA — it's where fish get it from. 100% plant-based and mercury-free.

Vitamin B12 Sublingual

💊 1 tablet daily · Dissolve under tongue
WhyVegetarian gap — B12 is almost exclusively found in meat and eggs. Deficiency causes fatigue and neurological issues.
Dose1,000mcg methylcobalamin daily
Best BrandJarrow Formulas Methyl B12 (cherry sublingual)
Buy AtiHerb, Amazon, Whole Foods, Vitamin Shoppe
Best TimeMorning — dissolve under tongue, hold 30 seconds before swallowing
⚠️ Important: Sublingual absorption bypasses the gut and is 3–10× more effective. Do not just swallow the tablet directly.

Vitamin D3

💊 1,000–2,000 IU daily · Morning with food
WhyNearly everyone is deficient indoors. Supports hormonal balance, thyroid health, and immune function.
Dose1,000–2,000 IU daily (confirm exact dose with OB-GYN after blood test)
Best BrandNatureWise Vitamin D3 2000 IU
Buy AtAmazon, Target, Costco (great value), Walmart
Best TimeMorning with breakfast — fat-soluble, needs food with fat to absorb
💡 Note: Take D3 with K2 if possible (many combos exist) — K2 helps direct calcium to bones rather than arteries.

CoQ10 (Ubiquinol)

💊 200mg daily · Morning or afternoon with food
WhyAntioxidant that supports egg quality and ovarian health. Especially important for preconception at your age.
Dose200mg daily (ubiquinol form is more bioavailable)
Best BrandQunol Ultra CoQ10 or Jarrow Formulas Ubiquinol
Buy AtCostco (Qunol is excellent value), Amazon, CVS
Best TimeWith your largest meal of the day — fat-soluble, always take with food
💡 Note: Choose ubiquinol over ubiquinone — it's the active, reduced form and 2× more absorbable, especially over age 25.
